Zoo Animals

Bears

  • Ollie

    • At our wonderful zoo, Ollie is the charismatic ambassador of the bear world. Ollie, a delightful American black bear, has a glossy black coat and a distinctive patch of white fur on his chest, making him easily recognizable. He's an active and inquisitive bear, often seen frolicking around his spacious habitat, climbing logs, and interacting with various enrichment toys. Ollie's engaging personality and curious nature make him a favorite attraction, offering visitors a chance to witness the beauty and intelligence of this incredible species up close

  • Mona

    • Introducing Mona, the enchanting resident of our zoo's bear exhibit. Mona, a captivating and rare Andean bear, stands out with her unique russet fur and striking facial markings. She is a sight to behold as she gracefully explores her forested enclosure, demonstrating her remarkable tree-climbing skills. Mona's presence serves as a reminder of the importance of conserving her endangered species and the fragile cloud forest habitats they call home. Visitors can observe Mona's grace and charm while learning about the conservation efforts dedicated to her species

Giraffes

  • Frankie

    • Frankie, a true star of our zoo's savannah, is an elegant and striking giraffe with distinctive irregular spots adorning his towering frame. His long, graceful neck and friendly disposition make him a favorite among visitors of all ages. Frankie can often be seen leisurely grazing on the lush vegetation in his habitat or reaching high into the trees to nibble on leaves. His presence provides a captivating opportunity to witness the majesty of giraffes up close and appreciate the beauty of these gentle giants.

  • Coconut

    • Coconut, the beloved resident of our zoo's giraffe exhibit, is a remarkable and endearing giraffe known for her creamy white coat and strikingly large, dark eyes. She is a graceful presence, known for her elegant gait as she roams her expansive habitat. Coconut's friendly nature and her penchant for interacting with visitors, especially during feeding times, make her a popular attraction. Her presence not only captivates guests but also educates them about the fascinating adaptations and behaviors of giraffes, making her a cherished member of our zoo family.

She's often found lounging in the sun, basking in her majestic surroundings, or occasionally showing her playful side with her pride. Mella represents the epitome of lioness strength and beauty, captivating visitors with her commanding presence.

He can often be seen surveying his domain from high perches or commanding attention with his resonant roars. Karl embodies the awe-inspiring nature of these apex predators and offers visitors a glimpse into the life of a true king of the jungle.

Monkeys

  • Cookie

    • Cookie, a playful capuchin monkey, brings boundless energy and mischief to our zoo's primate exhibit. With a tuft of white fur on her head, Cookie's agile antics never fail to delight visitors. She's known for her acrobatic displays and social interactions with fellow monkeys, offering an entertaining glimpse into the playful world of capuchins.

  • Earl

    • Earl, the wise and contemplative howler monkey, resides in our tranquil rainforest enclosure. His thick, black fur and distinctive vocalizations make him a unique and fascinating addition to our zoo. Earl's contemplative nature and haunting howls provide an auditory experience that transports visitors deep into the heart of the jungle.

  • Banana Pudding

    • Banana Pudding, a cheeky and inquisitive squirrel monkey, is the star of our tropical forest exhibit. With his vibrant yellow and white coat, he's always on the move, swinging through the trees, and exploring every nook and cranny of his habitat. Visitors are charmed by Banana Pudding's playful antics and his insatiable curiosity, giving them a taste of the lively world of squirrel monkeys

Alligators

  • Wren

    • Wren, a commanding American alligator, holds court in our reptile exhibit. With her armored, dark green scales and piercing eyes, Wren embodies the primal allure of these prehistoric reptiles. Visitors can observe her basking in the sun near the water's edge, showcasing the timeless power of American alligators.

  • Aspen

    • Aspen, a captivating albino alligator, stands out in our reptile habitat with her striking, pale appearance. Her ivory scales and pinkish eyes provide a mesmerizing contrast to the traditional alligator aesthetic. Aspen's unique beauty serves as a living testament to the incredible diversity within the alligator species, and her presence never fails to leave a lasting impression on zoo visitors.

  • Mika

    • Mika, our feisty Nile crocodile, adds a touch of intrigue to the reptile experience at our zoo. With her formidable build and the unmistakable V-shaped ridges on her snout, Mika embodies the raw power and cunning of Nile crocodiles. Visitors can witness her lurking beneath the water's surface, waiting for the perfect moment to spring into action, offering an exhilarating glimpse into the world of these ancient predators.
